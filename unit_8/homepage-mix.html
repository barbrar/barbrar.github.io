Asian American Studies Center Press

bridging research with community

Since 1970, the Asian American Studies Center Press has committed to publishing scholarship on the histories of Asian Americans and Pacific Islanders.

For more than 40 years, the publications arm of the Center has released a large collection of titles, including leading Asian American journals, almanacs, bibliographies, directories, anthologies, public policy reports, memoirs and definitive texts.
